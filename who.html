Who

The Code Kitty team formed at Twin Cities Startup Weekend: Education 2017 and went on to win it!

We now have a plan, a design, and a team of awesome people working hard to make it happen.

Our mission is to enable students to explore by empowering teachers. Our research and interviews with teachers have shown that the best way to do this is through Professional Development for teachers in the field of robotics. This would give teachers a new level of comfort with robotics and coding technologies, allowing them to lift the "No! Don't break it!" embargo on student access to technology.

When teachers know more, students win.
