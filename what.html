Meet Code Kitty!

Code Kitty is the product (and winner!) of Twin Cities Startup Weekend Edu 2017 hackathon.
A robot anyone can build, and everyone can afford.

Open, Extensible, and Ready to Explore

We are designing a very affordable Robotics kit around Open Standards. Leveraging the power of the Arduino development ecosystem, we are building a 3D printed, easily-hackable, user-personalized robot kit.

Robots for Everyone

And since we believe all students should have access to STEM technology learning opportunities, we are pricing the kit at $50, and publishing all of the chassis and part designs online, so anyone with access to a 3D printer can make or modify their own.

Not just another kit

We will then teach the teachers how to build and hack the robots, so that they are comfortable letting their students explore them.
